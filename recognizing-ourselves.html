Recognizing Ourselves

Everybody arrives with their own story, sometimes pointed down a difficult road, but always possible to turn around, until the last adaption, hopefully never arriving Responding to Manu Chao, "¿Cuando llegaré?" (When will I arrive?) "Ojala, jamas" (hopefully, never) Everything bad happens, but everything good does as well. — The key point of the manifestation of mind is that mind is not a property of the physical body, it's the fundamental quality of the universe. Symbolic relations generate space between possible extremes, existing beyond condensed forms, as proudly in-formation. Therefore, act like physical borders don't exist because they are only symptoms of that still in-formation. Every angle you see comes from entities too shy to say hi but directing from the periphery. I misspoke: you are them and they are you, there is no boundary. English is my second language, glossolalia is my first, and yours too.
