Clean water anywhere, and for everybody

The planet's water sources are no longer clean. While rumors of graphene-based water filtering and desalination have been around for decades, metal-organic frameworks, or MOFs, are emerging as an even more attractive solution to thirst: a novel molecular structure to passively collect water anywhere, directly from the atmosphere. Water Harvesting Inc. is developing an MOF that can do this at a rate of multiple liters/per day per kilogram of material. Watch a report from the channel Seeker on YouTube about this development. This is a fundamental game-changing technology to our conception of space within earth's atmosphere. We will be able to live anywhere without having to build infrastructure to bring clean water.
